THE PEATNUT BUTTER FALCON

DESCRIPTION ABOUT THE MOVIE:

The Peanut Butter Falcon is an adventure story set in the world of a modern Mark Twain that begins when Zak (22),a youngman with Down syndrome, runs away from the nursing home,where he lives to chase his dream of becoming a professional wrestler by attending the wrestling school The Salt Water Redneck.Together they experience life, drink whiskey, catch fish and Zak learns to both swim and shoot guns. When Eleanor (Dakota Johnson), a kind nurse accused of Zak's escape, finds them convincing her to join them on their journey.
